Ballon d’Or 2025: Full List of Nominees & Everything You Need to Know

By TrendNest, Contributor.

Published August 09, 2025, 01:00 PM

The Ballon d’Or is football’s most prestigious individual prize — and the 2025 nominees list is out. It mixes household superstars with young talent who broke through this season. If you follow football, this list is must-see.

Below you’ll find the full 30-player list, the main favorites, surprise entries, and everything else to know before the ceremony in September.

When and where is the Ballon d’Or 2025 ceremony?

The awards ceremony will take place on 22 September 2025 at the Théâtre du Châtelet in Paris. That’s when the winner will be revealed — until then, the debate and the speculation begin.

Who are the favorites?

A few names stand out on almost every fan and pundit list. Players like Jude Bellingham, Erling Haaland, and Kylian Mbappé are frequently mentioned as frontrunners thanks to consistent performances, big goals, and trophy-winning seasons.

These players carried their teams in high-pressure matches and left a clear mark on the season — which is exactly the kind of impact Ballon d’Or voters reward.

Surprises and breakthrough candidates

The list also includes rising stars and surprise names who grabbed attention this season. Players like Cole Palmer, Michael Olise, and Lamine Yamal earned their nominations by making big contributions at club level and showing high ceilings for the future.

Club representation & interesting stats

Several clubs are well-represented — notably Paris Saint-Germain, which has a large contingent among nominees. That reflects how success at club level (and deep runs in European competitions) tends to sway voters.

What voters consider

The Ballon d’Or is awarded based on season-long performance: trophies won, consistency, influence in important matches, and overall quality. Individual stats matter, but context (big performances in big games) carries huge weight.

How to follow the ceremony

Expect live coverage from major sports channels and online portals on the night of the ceremony. If you follow football closely, tune in to see reactions, expert analysis, and the big reveal.

Final thoughts

The Ballon d’Or nominees list gives us a snapshot of who dominated the football year — a mix of proven stars and the next generation breaking through. It’s always fun to debate, and this year’s 30-player list promises a lively contest.
